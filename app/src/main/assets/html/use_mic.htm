Using the
Remote Microphone

Once you have set up the connection to Dragon, tap the microphone icon to turn on the Dragon mic. You will now be able to dictate into Dragon using your mobile device as a wireless microphone:

Tap the microphone again to turn off the Dragon mic:

To make the microphone stop listening temporarily, say "Go to Sleep." To reactivate the microphone, say "Wake Up." Here's what the microphone looks like when you say "Go to sleep":

You cannot turn the Dragon Remote Microphone off by voice.

Notes: If you are dictating with the Dragon Remote Microphone Application and switch to another app on your smartphone or supported device, Dragon stops listening to what you say. When you return to the microphone app, you can continue dictating.
